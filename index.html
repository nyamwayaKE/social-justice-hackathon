About us

Vocal citizen is an online engagement platform whereby citizens can create petitions against their elected representatives.

Citizens can also create polls to guide them on matters that affect them, to try and come up with solutions.

It aims to also provide a platform whereby elected leaders can engage with the people they are representing.
